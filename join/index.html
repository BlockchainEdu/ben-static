Get Involved

A community of over 2,350 students and graduates across the world.

Student Memberships are Sponsored: BEN sets aside a portion of our funds to acommodate for free student memberships.

Sponsorship: Think your organization might be interested in sponsorship? Please email us at contact@blockchainedu.org

Membership

Students

You are a currently enrolled undergraduate, masters, phd, or high school student. Valid .edu email required. Alumni and faculty/staff are not included in this membership type.


Benefits
  • Free/discounted admission to BEN events and partner events
  • Networking opportunities
  • Access to BEN Playbook and educational material
Annual Cost: FREE

Young Professionals

You are a recent graduate, professor, educator that wants to continue your blockchain education and continue being part of the BEN community.


Benefits
  • Free/discounted admission to BEN events and partner events
  • Access to private BEN Slack & Facebook Group
  • Networking opportunities
  • Access to BEN Playbook and educational material
Annual Cost: $120

Learn more
Blockchain Clubs

You are a university or high school blockchain club that wants to receive support from BEN.


Benefits
  • Free/discounted admission to BEN events and partner events
  • Access to private BEN Slack & Facebook Group
  • Access to BEN Playbook, all lesson plans, and educational material
  • Office hours/regular calls with BEN 1-on-1 to help you with your events.
  • Sponsor / speaker intros.
Annual Cost: FREE
