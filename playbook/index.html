The best educational content put together and refined over the course of four years by BEN leaders, students, alumni, partners, and supporters.

Mass adoption is taking too long. So BEN is starting a fun little worldwide game called “let’s make mass adoption happen.” If you want to play, this playbook is your guide.

You may be a developer, business leader, researcher, mathematician, economist, computer scientist, law student, political scientist, philosopher, or something in between. With all of your help, we will use blockchain to make a real impact on society.

Here you will find endless guides for everything from learning about blockchain to throwing blockchain-related events to traveling to blockchain conferences. Everything you could ever want to become a leader in this space is now at your reach.
