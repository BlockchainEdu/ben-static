Partners

BEN partners with blockchain companies and conferences to distribute educational material and opportunities to students. Email team@blockchainedu.org for more information

Educational Partnership

Companies may commission a course that would go into our curriculum. We encourage our students to do weith things, like launching smart contracts, running nodes, messing around with tokens, and more. BEN tracks students' progress and completions with the issuance of certificates of completion. BEN can deliver you valuable metrics, feedback, and insights from students.

BEN Fundraiser Sponsorship

BEN is planning fundraisers in 2019 that companies can sponsor. The fundraisers are done in cryptocurrency and thus people get practice actually using wallets and tokens at our events.

Event Partnership

BEN will share your event with our students and get a higher student presence for your event.
