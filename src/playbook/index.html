BEN Playbook

The BEN Playbook PDF is a 40+ page PDF issued to every BEN student that aggregates together our four years of insights running events, teaching curriculum into a cohesive initiative with an incentive structure that runs all the way from a student first joining BEN to joining a company or starting their own project.

Playbook Contents


Introduction - intro to BEN, how BEN started, what BEN students can expect out of BEN, and how to use the Playbook.

Level 1 Plays - plays to learn about blockchain.

Level 2 Plays - plays to learn how to develop for blockchain projects.

Level 3 Plays - plays to learn how to throw blockchain-related events on campus.

Travel - guides on how to stay safe while traveling, get deals on flights and lodging, tips for international travel and the visa application process, and a list of BEN partner conferences.

Available Plays


Two plays from our BEN playbook are available to the public, Blockchain 101 and Do a Bitcoin Airdrop. For students who want access to the rest of the plays, join BEN.

Purchase a Page


Companies may purchase a page in our Playbook for a play related to that blockchain project. We encourage weird and wacky plays, like launching smart contracts, running nodes, messing around with tokens, and more. BEN tracks students' progress and completions with the issuance of certificates of completion. BEN can deliver you valuable metrics, feedback, and insights from students.
