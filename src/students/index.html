Student BENefits

Community

Be a part of a growing, non-profit grassroots organization run by students and graduates for students and graduates. Access to an online community of over 2,350 plus students and recent graduates from around the world. Region channels for your country, region, and even city.

Events

Free/discounted tickets to BEN partner conferences, workshops, hackathons, and bootcamps. Also access to BEN hosted events like airdrops, Blockchain Madness, Blockchain Education Month, Blockchain Startup Gauntlet, Hackathons, and AMAs with CEOs, founders, developers, and teams,

Opportunities

Opportunities to volunteer and intern, so you can build resume/portfolio and grow your network. Get matched with BEN partner companies based on your interests, preferences, and even work style (full-time, part-time, local work, remote work)
